Demon

Origination: Most likely Paleolithic Ages

Gender: Usually male, sometimes female

Description: Usually like an angel, but with wings like a bats', and fiery

---------------------------------------------------------------------

Said by most paranormal experts, demons are usually the hardest to understand and deal with. They

are very cruel, and are said to have an anger like no other ghost. In Christianity, they were

said to rebel against God, or they were cast out of heaven. The only other place they could

work for was Hell. They are cursed to "walk all over the world", so they can appear anywhere.

---------------------------------------------------------------------

Appearance type: Mainly full body.
